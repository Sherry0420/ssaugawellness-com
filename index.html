About Holistic Nutrition

The term "Holistic" refers to the concept that a subject should be studied as a whole and not just a sum of its parts. According to Holistic Wellness credo, an individual's mind, body, emotions and environment must be taken into consideration when addressing any health concern. A holistic nutritionist focuses on a natural approach to a your health concerns by customizing a healthy diet and lifestyle that suits. When I meet my client, I do not address your health issues isolated and I certainly do not offer pill popping solutions. My team and I address the root cause of your health concerns by taking a natural approach. This natural approach incorporates emotional, spiritual and physical health to create a state of well being for optimum health. After meeting my clients, I go through their family history, their current lifestyle, emotional, spiritual and physical state. We focus on 3 main health concerns and I customize a plan especially for that individual, keeping in mind all the preferences and constraints that my client have. So the lifestyle changes in your plan can be adopted for life and this provide positive health improvements that are beneficial for generations to come.
