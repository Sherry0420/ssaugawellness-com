About Holistic Nutrition

The term "Holistic" refers to the concept that a subject should be studied as a whole and not just a sum of its parts. According to Holistic Wellness credo, an individual's mind, body, emotions and environment must be taken into consideration when addressing any health concern. A holistic nutritionist focuses on a natural approach to a your health concerns by customizing a healthy diet and lifestyle that suits you. When I meet my client, I do not address your health issues isolated and I certainly do not offer pill popping solutions. My team and I strive to investigate the root cause of your health issue by taking a natural approach. This natural approach incorporates emotional, spiritual and physical healing to create a state of well being for your optimum health.

MBCT

MBCT is Mindfulness-based cognitive therapy. It incorporates meditation, breathing exercises, and other elements of mindfulness along with cognitive behavior therapy. This approach is proven to have positive impact on physical and mental health.
