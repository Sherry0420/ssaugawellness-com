Simple Process

30 Minutes Inital Conusltation

In my introductory appointment we discuss your family history as well as your personal medical history. We ask you to shortlist 3 major health concerns to be addressed in our 90 day action plan.

90 Minutes of In person Consultation/Action Plan

In this in person appointment we sit together to go through the process of findong the root cause of your symptoms. At the end of this detailed appointment I leave you with a detailed action plan on how to bring your mind, body and spirit in homeostasis.

60 Minutes of Followup Appointment/progress

This appointment is to access our success and to re-evaluate what works for you and what dosen't. We decide at this point if you are satisfied with the progress or would you like to address another health or wellness milestone.
